UG Admissions (TNEA)

Admission Quota

As per TNEA, 65% of admissions are done through government quota and 35% of students are admitted under management counselling. A mere pass in HSC or its equivalent with the minimum average percentage in the relevant subjects is required. A pass in any one of the HSC (Vocational Stream) prescribed for B.E Degree admissions, or any other equivalent qualification approved by the State Board of Technical Education and Training / Higher Secondary Board of Tamilnadu / Anna University, is also acceptable.

Qualifying Examination Marks

Maths – 100 marks + Physics & Chemistry – 100 marks = Total: 200 marks (Cut-off)

Minimum Eligibility – HSC (Academic Stream)

  • For Other communities: Minimum 55% in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry.
  • For BC / BC(Muslim) communities: Minimum 50% in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry.
  • For MBC & DNC communities: Minimum 45% in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry.
  • For SC/ST candidates: A mere pass in the qualifying examination.

Minimum Eligibility – HSC (Vocational Stream)

  • For Other communities: Minimum 55% in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry.
  • For BC / BC(Muslim) communities: Minimum 50% in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry.
  • For MBC & DNC communities: Minimum 45% in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry.

PG Admissions (TANCET)

Admission Process

University Departments / University Colleges of Anna University, Government and Government Aided Engineering Colleges and Arts & Science Colleges and Self-financing Colleges in Tamil Nadu will be admitted through the TANCET exam conducted by Anna University.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must fulfill the following criteria. They must have cleared any one of the following Bachelor's degree or equivalent, at the relevant field with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for reserved category candidates):

  • B.E / B.Tech. / B.Arch. degree programme.
  • B.Pharm. / B.Sc (Agri. / Forestry / Horticulture / Fishery) degree programme.
  • Master's Degree in the relevant branch of Science / Arts.
  • M.Sc. (5 years) Integrated Degree Programme.
  • M.Sc. 3 years (Computer Science / IT) and M.Sc. 2 years (Computer Science / IT).
  • MCA (3 years) after 10+2+3 years bachelor's degree.
  • MCA Lateral Entry (2 years) after 10+2+3 years degree in BCA or B.Sc. (CS/IT).
  • Candidates with 10+2+AMIE OR 10+3 years diploma + AMIE are also eligible to apply.

Common FAQs

Is there a lateral entry scheme?

Yes, 20% of the sanctioned intake in each branch is admitted in the 2nd year B.E./B.Tech programs through the lateral entry scheme.

What is the PG minimum marks?

Minimum 50% in bachelor's degree (45% for reserved categories).

How many UG seats are available?

The institute currently has 420+ sanctioned UG seats across 8 disciplines.
