Entrepreneurship Development Cell

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) is established at GCEE(IRTT) in the year 2013 with AICTE grant in order to develop Entrepreneurial skills of students and faculty members.

The following programmes have been organized under EDC for students and faculty members.
